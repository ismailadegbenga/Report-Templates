Normal
------



Bilateral tubal blockage
------------------------
Indication: Primary infertility

Comparison Study: Nil

Technique: Retrograde injection of gastrografin through Wilkinson's cannula anchored at cervix and AP exposures of pelvis obtained.

Findings:

Contrast flowed freely into the uterine cavity revealing a moderate capacity endometrial cavity with a smooth and regular outline.

The isthmus of both fallopian tubes appear normal. The ampulla of both fallopian tubes were moderately dilated with abrupt cut-off and no intra-peritoneal spillage of contrast.

Impression: Bilateral tubal blockage
